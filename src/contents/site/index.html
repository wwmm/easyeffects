Easy Effects

Easy Effects applies audio effects to applications managed by PipeWire.
You can apply effects to application output or to the microphone before sending its audio to a recording application.

These are the Easy Effects help pages. Here you can find information concerning usage of the app, such as descriptions of each audio effect.

Additional information not directly related to the app (e.g., how to build Easy Effects) is kept in a separate wiki:

Note that the documentation inside the app is static and only updated when the app is updated.
The online documentation is automatically updated for each release, so it may be newer.
